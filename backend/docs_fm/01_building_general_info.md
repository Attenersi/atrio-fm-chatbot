# Meridian Business Center — General Information

## Building Overview

Meridian Business Center is a modern 6-floor commercial office building located at Graanstraat 12, 4811 JD Breda, Netherlands. The building was constructed in 2017 and underwent a renovation of the lobby and common areas in 2023. Total gross floor area is 8,200 m².

The building is owned by Vastgoed Meridian B.V. and managed by VanDalen Facility Services. The on-site facility management team is available Monday through Friday.

## Floors and Layout

- Floor 0 (Ground floor): Main lobby, reception desk, mailroom, loading dock, bicycle parking (indoor), 2 meeting rooms (Rooms G01, G02), café/canteen
- Floor 1: Office suites 101–112, shared kitchen, print room, 1 meeting room (Room 113)
- Floor 2: Office suites 201–212, shared kitchen, print room, 1 meeting room (Room 213)
- Floor 3: Office suites 301–312, shared kitchen, print room, 2 meeting rooms (Rooms 313, 314)
- Floor 4: Office suites 401–410, server room (Room 411), shared kitchen, print room, 1 meeting room (Room 413)
- Floor 5: Rooftop terrace (accessible May–September), mechanical room (restricted access), solar panel array

Total rentable office suites: 46
Total meeting rooms: 7
Total occupancy capacity: approximately 520 persons

## After-Hours Access

Tenants with an active access badge can enter the building 24/7 through the side entrance on Parkstraat. The main entrance is locked outside operating hours. After-hours access requires a valid badge — contact reception to request badge activation for after-hours use. There is no additional charge for after-hours access.

Emergency exits remain functional at all times and must never be blocked.

# Fire Safety & Emergency Procedures

## Fire Safety Systems

Meridian Business Center is equipped with the following fire safety systems:
- Automatic fire detection: Smoke detectors in every room, hallway, and common area (Bosch FPA-5000 system)
- Fire alarm pull stations: Located at every stairwell entrance on every floor (red boxes)
- Sprinkler system: Wet pipe sprinklers throughout the building (except server room which uses FM-200 gas suppression)
- Fire extinguishers: Located at every floor landing, in kitchens, and near electrical panels
- Emergency lighting: Battery-backed LED fixtures along all exit routes
- Fire doors: Self-closing fire-rated doors at stairwell entrances and between fire compartments
- Smoke extraction: Automatic smoke vents in stairwells, activated by fire alarm
- PA system: Building-wide public address system for emergency announcements

## Fire Extinguisher Locations and Types

Each floor has the following extinguishers:
- Near stairwell A (east): 1x ABC powder extinguisher (red), 1x CO2 extinguisher (black band)
- Near stairwell B (west): 1x ABC powder extinguisher (red), 1x CO2 extinguisher (black band)
- Kitchen: 1x wet chemical extinguisher (yellow band) — for cooking oil fires only
- Near electrical panels: 1x CO2 extinguisher (black band) — for electrical fires

Use the correct extinguisher type:
- Paper, wood, textiles → ABC powder (red) or water mist
- Electrical equipment → CO2 (black band) — NEVER water
- Cooking oil → Wet chemical (yellow band) — NEVER water
- Flammable liquids → ABC powder or CO2

## Evacuation Procedure

When the fire alarm sounds (continuous high-pitched tone):
1. Stop what you are doing immediately
2. Do not collect personal belongings
3. Close windows if you can do so quickly (do not lock doors)
4. Walk calmly to the nearest stairwell — DO NOT use elevators
5. Follow the green exit signs
6. Descend to the ground floor and exit through the nearest emergency exit
7. Proceed to the assembly point: the parking area on Parkstraat (marked with green signs)
8. Report to your company's floor warden at the assembly point
9. Do not re-enter the building until the all-clear is given by the fire brigade or facility management

## Assembly Point

The primary assembly point is the Parkstraat parking area, east side of the building. The secondary assembly point (if Parkstraat is blocked) is the Graanstraat sidewalk, west side.

## Fire Drills

Fire drills are conducted twice per year (typically March and October). All tenants are required to participate. You will receive an email notification 1 week before the drill with instructions. The drill typically takes 15–20 minutes.

## If You Discover a Fire

1. Activate the nearest fire alarm pull station (break glass, push button)
2. Call 112 immediately
3. If the fire is small and you are trained, attempt to extinguish it with the appropriate extinguisher
4. If the fire is large, spreading, or producing heavy smoke — evacuate immediately
5. Close doors behind you to contain the fire
6. Alert others as you evacuate
7. Do not use elevators
8. Call the building emergency hotline: +31 (0)76 555 0199

## Smoke Detector Alerts

If a smoke detector in your suite is beeping intermittently (short beeps every 30–60 seconds), this usually means a low battery or sensor issue — not an actual fire. Report it to facility management for inspection. Do not disconnect or cover smoke detectors — this is a violation of fire safety regulations and Dutch law.

If the continuous building-wide alarm sounds, treat it as a real emergency and evacuate.

## Medical Emergency

1. Call 112 for ambulance
2. If trained, provide first aid or use the AED
3. Call reception (+31 76 555 0100) so they can direct paramedics to the correct location
4. Do not move an injured person unless they are in immediate danger
5. Send someone to meet paramedics at the main entrance

## Prohibited Items and Activities

The following are strictly prohibited in the building:
- Open flames (candles, incense, gas burners) — except in the designated café kitchen
- Smoking anywhere inside the building (including e-cigarettes) — designated smoking area is outside the Parkstraat entrance
- Storing flammable liquids, gases, or chemicals in office suites
- Blocking fire exits, corridors, or stairwell doors with furniture or equipment
- Disabling, covering, or disconnecting smoke detectors
- Tampering with fire extinguishers or fire alarm pull stations
- Using barbecues or grills on the rooftop terrace

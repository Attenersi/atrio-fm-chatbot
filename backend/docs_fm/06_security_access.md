# Security & Access Control

## Access Badge System

The building uses an AEOS access control system (Nedap) with contactless RFID badges. Each tenant employee receives a personal access badge that provides access to:
- Main entrance (during building hours 07:00–21:00)
- Side entrance on Parkstraat (24/7)
- Your company's floor
- Shared facilities (kitchens, bathrooms, meeting rooms on your floor)
- Parking garage (if included in your lease)

Badges do NOT provide access to:
- Other companies' office suites
- Server room (Floor 4, Room 411)
- Mechanical rooms (restricted to FM team)
- Electrical closets (restricted to FM team)

## Requesting New Badges

To request a new badge for a new employee:
1. Send an email to reception@meridian-breda.nl with the employee's full name, company name, and start date
2. Allow 2 business days for badge programming
3. Badge pickup at reception — employee must show valid ID (passport, Dutch ID card, or driver's license)
4. Cost: €25 per badge (billed to company service charge)

## Lost or Stolen Badges

Report a lost or stolen badge immediately to reception (+31 76 555 0100) or email reception@meridian-breda.nl. The lost badge will be deactivated within 1 hour during business hours or immediately for after-hours emergency calls.

Replacement badge: €25 (same process as new badge request).

If you suspect your badge was stolen, also report this to the police and inform facility management — we may need to review security camera footage.

## Deactivating Badges

When an employee leaves your company, notify reception within 24 hours. We will deactivate the badge. Return the physical badge to reception. Unreturned badges are charged at €50 per badge at lease reconciliation.

## Visitor Policy

All visitors must check in at the reception desk. The process:
1. Visitor arrives at main entrance and goes to reception
2. Reception calls the host (tenant contact person) to confirm
3. Visitor receives a temporary visitor badge (valid for the day only)
4. Visitor must return the badge to reception when leaving
5. Visitors are not permitted in the building unescorted after 18:00

For large events or groups of visitors (5+ people):
- Notify reception at least 2 business days in advance
- Provide a list of visitor names
- Designate a host who will meet and escort the group

## Delivery and Contractor Access

Delivery drivers and contractors check in at reception and receive a temporary contractor badge (orange lanyard). Contractors must:
- Sign in and out at reception
- Wear the contractor badge visibly at all times
- Be escorted by FM staff when accessing restricted areas
- Provide proof of insurance and certifications for specialized work (electrical, HVAC)

Regular delivery services (PostNL, DHL, UPS) are known to reception and have streamlined check-in procedures.

## Security Cameras

The building has CCTV cameras in the following locations:
- Main entrance and lobby
- Side entrance (Parkstraat)
- Parking garage (all levels)
- Loading dock
- Elevator lobbies on each floor
- Stairwell entrances
- Rooftop terrace entrance

Cameras do NOT record inside office suites, bathrooms, or kitchens. Footage is stored for 30 days in compliance with GDPR (AVG) regulations. Access to footage requires a legitimate reason (theft, incident investigation) and is managed by facility management. Requests must be made in writing.

## After-Hours Security

There is no on-site security guard after hours. The building is monitored remotely by Securitas between 21:00–07:00. In case of a security alarm (unauthorized access attempt, forced door):
1. Securitas receives the alert automatically
2. They contact the facility manager
3. If needed, police are dispatched

If you are working late and notice anything suspicious (unauthorized person, forced door, broken window), call:
- Building emergency hotline: +31 (0)76 555 0199
- Police: 0900-8844 (non-emergency) or 112 (emergency)

## Key Management

Physical keys exist only for:
- Office suite entrance doors (each tenant receives 2 keys; additional keys: €35 each)
- Supply closets (FM team only)
- Restricted areas (FM team only)

Do not make copies of keys — this is prohibited per your lease agreement. All key requests must go through facility management.

## Tailgating Policy

Do not hold doors open for people you don't recognize. Each person should badge in individually. This is especially important at the Parkstraat entrance during after-hours access. If someone asks you to hold the door, politely ask them to use their own badge.

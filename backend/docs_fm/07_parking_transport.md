# Parking & Transportation

## Car Parking

Meridian Business Center has an underground parking garage with 2 levels:
- Level P1: 45 spots (including 8 EV charging, 3 disabled, 2 visitor)
- Level P2: 40 spots (standard only)
- Total: 85 spots

Parking is allocated per tenant company based on lease agreements. Contact your company's office manager to check if you have an assigned spot or access to the shared pool.

Parking access: Use your building access badge at the garage gate on Parkstraat. The gate opens automatically. If your badge doesn't work, check with reception — parking access must be activated separately.

Parking rates (for tenants with garage access):
- Reserved spot: €125/month (billed to company)
- Shared pool access: €75/month (first come, first served)
- No hourly or daily tenant parking option — it's monthly only

## Visitor Parking

2 visitor spots are available on Level P1 (spots P01 and P02, marked "VISITOR"). Visitors must register at reception upon arrival. Maximum stay: 4 hours. For longer visits, use the public parking garage at Markendaalseweg (5-minute walk).

## Disabled Parking

3 accessible parking spots on Level P1 (spots P03–P05), closest to the elevator. These are reserved for persons with a valid European disabled parking card (Europese Gehandicaptenparkeerkaart). The card must be visible on the dashboard.

## EV Charging

8 EV charging stations on Level P1 (spots P45–P52):
- Charger type: 22 kW AC, Type 2 connector
- Cost: €0.30/kWh, billed monthly
- How to use: Plug in → tap building badge on charger → charging starts
- Badge must be registered for EV charging at reception (1 business day processing)
- Do not park at EV spots without charging — cars will be asked to move
- Maximum charging time: 8 hours per session

If all chargers are occupied, the reception desk maintains a waiting list. You'll get a notification when a charger becomes available.

## Bicycle Parking

Indoor bicycle parking is on the ground floor, accessible via the Parkstraat entrance:
- Capacity: 120 bicycles
- Access: Building badge required
- Free for all building users
- Covered, dry, and lit
- No reserved spots — first come, first served
- E-bike charging outlets: 6 standard outlets available along the wall (bring your own charger)

There are also 20 outdoor bicycle racks at the main entrance on Graanstraat for short-term parking or visitors.

Rules:
- Lock your bicycle even inside the indoor parking (theft is your responsibility)
- Do not park bicycles in hallways, stairwells, or near emergency exits
- Remove all personal bicycles before the annual deep clean (announced 2 weeks in advance)
- Abandoned bicycles (not moved in 3+ months) are tagged and removed after 2 weeks notice

## Public Transport

Breda Centraal Station is an 8-minute walk from the building:
- Walk: North exit → Willemstraat → Graanstraat (800m)
- NS trains to Rotterdam (30 min), Amsterdam (70 min), Eindhoven (40 min), Tilburg (15 min)
- Arriva buses stop at Stationslaan (3-minute walk)

OV-fiets (public rental bikes) are available at Breda Centraal Station for €4.55/24h with a personal OV-chipkaart.

## Taxi / Ride-hailing

Drop-off/pickup point: Graanstraat, in front of the main entrance. There is a 10-minute parking zone for taxis and ride-hailing services. Do not block the loading dock entrance.

## Loading Dock

The loading dock is on the Parkstraat side (ground floor):
- Operating hours: Monday–Friday 07:00–18:00
- Maximum vehicle size: 7.5 ton truck
- Booking required: Contact reception at least 24 hours in advance
- Duration: Maximum 1 hour per booking
- Access: Ground floor hallway, door marked "Loading Dock" (badge required)

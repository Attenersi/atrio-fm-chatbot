## How to Book

Meeting rooms are booked through the building's shared calendar system:
1. Email reception@meridian-breda.nl with: room preference, date, time, duration, and number of attendees
2. Reception confirms availability within 1 hour during business hours
3. You receive a calendar invitation

Booking rules:
- Minimum booking: 30 minutes
- Maximum booking: 4 hours (full day bookings require FM approval)
- Cancellation: Cancel at least 2 hours before the scheduled time
- No-shows: If the room is not occupied within 15 minutes of the booking start time, it is automatically released
- Fair use: Maximum 2 bookings per company per day (additional bookings subject to availability)

## Meeting Room Equipment

All meeting rooms have:
- Display screen with HDMI and USB-C input
- Wireless screen sharing: Connect to "Meridian-Room-[number]" via AirPlay or Miracast
- High-definition webcam (Logitech Rally) for video conferencing
- Conference speakerphone (Jabra Speak 750) in larger rooms
- Whiteboard with markers and eraser (please clean after use)
- Power outlets at the table
- WiFi access (Meridian-Tenant and Meridian-Guest)

Rooms do NOT have:
- Projectors (screens are used instead)
- Computers — bring your own laptop
- Printers — use the floor's print room

## Meeting Room Issues

**"The display doesn't work"**
1. Check if the display is powered on (indicator light on the bottom edge)
2. Check if your laptop is connected (HDMI cable, USB-C cable, or wireless)
3. For wireless: connect to "Meridian-Room-[number]" in your WiFi settings
4. If the screen is black or shows "No Signal," unplug and replug the HDMI cable
5. Still not working? Contact reception for IT support

**"The room is too hot/cold"**
Meeting rooms have their own thermostats near the door. Adjust within the allowed range (19–24°C). If the thermostat is not responding, contact FM.

**"The previous meeting left a mess"**
Report to reception. Rooms should be left clean after use (whiteboard erased, cups removed, chairs pushed in).

## Rooftop Terrace

The rooftop terrace (Floor 5) is a shared relaxation space, open May through September:
- Hours: Monday–Friday 08:00–20:00, Saturday 10:00–18:00
- Seating: 30 persons (tables and chairs)
- Amenities: Sun shades, 4 power outlets, WiFi coverage
- Access: Building badge required (elevator to floor 5 or stairwell)

Terrace rules:
- No BBQs, grills, or open flames
- No glass bottles (use plastic or cans)
- No loud music (speakers) — use headphones
- Do not move furniture to the edge railing
- Clean up after yourself — bins available on the terrace
- Smoking is not permitted on the terrace
- The terrace is closed during high winds (above 60 km/h) or storms — FM will lock access

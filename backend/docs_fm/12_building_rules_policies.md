# Building Rules & Policies

## House Rules (Huishoudelijk Reglement)

All tenants and their employees, visitors, and contractors must comply with these rules. Violation of house rules may result in warnings and, for repeated offenses, escalation to the building owner.

### Noise
- Office suites are acoustically separated but not soundproof. Keep noise to reasonable office levels.
- Do not play music without headphones in common areas.
- Construction, drilling, or loud work within your suite: only permitted Monday–Friday 18:00–07:00 or weekends, with 1-week advance notice to FM and adjacent suites.
- Excessive noise complaints are logged. Repeated issues will be addressed with the tenant company's contact person.

### Smoking
- Smoking (including e-cigarettes and vaping) is strictly prohibited inside the building, including bathrooms, stairwells, and the rooftop terrace.
- Designated smoking area: outside the Parkstraat entrance (marked with signage and ash bin).
- Smoking within 5 meters of any entrance door is not permitted.

### Pets
- Pets are generally not allowed in the building.
- Exception: Certified assistance dogs (hulphonden with valid certification) are always welcome.
- Tenants wishing to bring a dog to their office must request written permission from FM and obtain consent from adjacent suites.

### Modifications to Office Suites
- Tenants may not make structural modifications (walls, floors, ceilings, windows, doors) without written approval from the building owner.
- Allowed without approval: hanging pictures/whiteboards (using approved wall hooks only — no drilling), rearranging furniture, adding desk plants.
- Not allowed: painting walls, installing additional lighting, modifying electrical outlets, installing window film, attaching anything to exterior windows.
- At lease end, suites must be returned in original condition (normal wear and tear excepted).

### Furniture in Common Areas
- Do not move or remove furniture from common areas (lobby, hallways, kitchens, meeting rooms).
- Do not place personal furniture in common areas (hallways, stairwells, near elevators).
- Storage in hallways is not permitted — this violates fire safety regulations.

### Kitchen Etiquette
- Clean up after yourself immediately. Dirty dishes left in the sink overnight will be discarded.
- Refrigerator is cleaned every Friday at 16:00. Label your items with name and date. Unlabeled items are discarded.
- Microwave: cover food when heating. Clean up any spills immediately.
- Report broken appliances to reception — do not attempt repairs.

### Deliveries and Moving
- All moves (furniture, large equipment) must be coordinated with FM at least 1 week in advance.
- Use Elevator B (larger) for all large items. Protective blankets will be installed.
- Moving is only permitted Monday–Friday 18:00–07:00 or weekends (to minimize disruption).
- Do not drag furniture across floors — use dollies or trolleys (available from FM).

## Service Charge

The monthly service charge covers:
- Cleaning of common areas
- HVAC operation and maintenance
- Elevator maintenance
- Lighting in common areas
- Water supply and waste water
- Waste collection and disposal
- Insurance (building structure)
- Fire safety systems maintenance
- Security monitoring (after-hours)
- Grounds maintenance
- Building management (FM team)

The service charge does NOT cover:
- Electricity usage within individual office suites (separately metered)
- Internet usage (included for WiFi users; dedicated lines are tenant expense)
- Parking (separate agreement)
- Meeting room catering
- Office suite cleaning beyond standard daily service
- Key and badge replacements

Service charge reconciliation happens annually in January. Tenants receive a statement comparing budgeted vs. actual costs, with any over/underpayment settled.

## Insurance

The building owner (Vastgoed Meridian B.V.) insures the building structure and common areas. Tenants are responsible for insuring their own contents, inventory, and liability (bedrijfsaansprakelijkheidsverzekering). Proof of insurance may be requested during lease renewal.

## Complaints Procedure

If you have a complaint about building services, other tenants, or facilities:
1. First: Report to facility management via email (maintenance@meridian-breda.nl) or the Atrio chatbot
2. FM will acknowledge within 1 business day and provide an expected resolution timeline
3. If not resolved satisfactorily: Escalate to the FM manager Jeroen van Dijk (+31 76 555 0101)
4. If still unresolved: Submit a formal complaint in writing to the building owner via eigenaar@meridian-breda.nl
5. Formal complaints are reviewed within 5 business days

## Data Privacy

The building collects and processes limited personal data in compliance with GDPR (AVG):
- Access badge records (who entered, when, which door) — retained for 90 days
- CCTV footage — retained for 30 days
- Visitor registration data — retained for 30 days
- Maintenance request records — retained for duration of lease + 2 years

Data controller: Vastgoed Meridian B.V. Privacy policy available at reception or by email request. Data protection inquiries: privacy@meridian-breda.nl.

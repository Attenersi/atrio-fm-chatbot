# Frequently Asked Questions — Quick Answers

## Lost and Found

Lost items are collected and stored at the reception desk on the ground floor. Items are kept for 30 days. After 30 days, unclaimed items are donated or disposed of. To check for a lost item, visit reception or email reception@meridian-breda.nl with a description of the item and approximate date/location where it was lost.

## Gym / Fitness

There is no gym or fitness facility in the building. Nearby options include:
- Basic-Fit Breda Centrum (Ginnekenweg 72, 5-minute walk)
- TrainMore Breda (Stationsplein 1, at Breda Centraal, 8-minute walk)
Reception can provide directions to these facilities.

## Asbestos

Meridian Business Center was built in 2017 and is completely asbestos-free. Dutch buildings constructed after 1994 are required to be asbestos-free. If you are planning suite renovations, you do not need an asbestos survey for this building. For renovation approval, contact facility management.

## Insurance Claims (Tenant Property Damage)

If your property was damaged due to a building issue (water leak, power surge, etc.):
1. Document the damage with photos immediately
2. Report the incident to facility management (maintenance@meridian-breda.nl)
3. Contact your own business insurance provider to file a claim
4. FM will provide an incident report upon written request (allow 5 business days)
5. Equipment model numbers or building specifications needed for your claim can be requested from FM

Facility management does not process insurance claims directly — they are handled between your insurer and the building owner's insurer (Vastgoed Meridian B.V.).

## Dress Code

There is no building-wide dress code. The rooftop terrace, lobby, and common areas have no specific clothing requirements beyond basic public decency. Individual tenant companies set their own dress codes for their office suites.

## Subletting

Subletting of office suites is not managed by facility management. For subletting inquiries, contact the property manager: Vestia Vastgoedbeheer, property@vestia-breda.nl, +31 (0)76 555 0200. Your lease agreement contains the specific subletting terms.

## Building Ownership / Sale

Questions about building ownership, potential sales, or property transactions are not handled by facility management. Contact the property manager (Vestia Vastgoedbeheer) or the building owner directly at eigenaar@meridian-breda.nl.

## Phone Signal / Mobile Coverage

The building does not have a mobile signal booster. Mobile reception varies by carrier and location within the building. Floor 4 and the parking garage tend to have weaker signal. For reliable calls, use WiFi calling (most modern phones support this) on the Meridian-Tenant network, or use the Meridian-Guest network for visitors. The building's VoIP QoS settings prioritize voice traffic on WiFi.

## Defibrillator (AED) Locations

Two AEDs are available in the building:
- Ground floor lobby, next to the elevator
- Floor 3 hallway, near meeting rooms 313/314

AEDs are maintained and inspected monthly by facility management. If an AED is used during a medical emergency, report it to FM immediately so the unit can be serviced and replacement pads/battery can be ordered.

## Food Trucks / External Catering Events

Food trucks and external catering in the parking area or loading dock require:
1. Written approval from facility management (minimum 2 weeks advance notice)
2. Proof of food safety certification (from the operator)
3. Proof of liability insurance (from the operator)
4. Coordination with reception for parking/access
Open flames and BBQs are not permitted on building grounds or the rooftop terrace.

## Bicycle Repair

There is no dedicated bike repair station in the building. The nearest bicycle repair shop is Fietswinkel Breda (Boschstraat 45, 10-minute walk). For flat tires, basic tools (pump, tire levers) are available at reception upon request.

## Noise from Neighboring Suites

If noise from an adjacent suite is disrupting your work:
1. First, try to resolve it directly with the neighboring tenant
2. If unresolved, report to facility management with specific details (time, duration, type of noise)
3. FM will mediate and may perform a noise assessment
4. Construction/drilling by other tenants is restricted to Mon-Fri 18:00-07:00 or weekends
5. Persistent violations are escalated to the building owner

## Pregnancy / Chemical Sensitivity

If you have health concerns about cleaning products, paint, or other chemicals used in the building:
1. Contact facility management with your specific concern
2. FM can coordinate with the cleaning company (Hago) to use alternative products in your area
3. Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) for all cleaning products are available upon request
4. Low-VOC paint options are available for scheduled painting work — request in advance

## Reporting Suspicious Activity

If you notice something suspicious (unauthorized person, unattended package, forced door, unusual behavior):
- During business hours: Call reception (+31 76 555 0100) or FM (+31 76 555 0101)
- After hours: Call the building emergency hotline (+31 76 555 0199)
- Immediate danger: Call 112
- Do not confront the person yourself
- If possible, note a description (appearance, direction of movement, time)
- For unattended packages: do not touch or move the item, keep distance, alert others in the area

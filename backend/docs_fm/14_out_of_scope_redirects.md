# Topics Not Handled by Facility Management

## Lease Rent and Contracts

Lease, rent payments, lease renewals, termination, and contract terms are handled by the property manager. Contact: Vestia Vastgoedbeheer, property@vestia-breda.nl, +31 (0)76 555 0200.

## Building Ownership Questions

Questions about building ownership, potential sales, or property transactions. Contact: building owner, eigenaar@meridian-breda.nl.

## Service Charge Billing Disputes

Disputes about service charge amounts or billing errors. Contact: property manager, Vestia Vastgoedbeheer, property@vestia-breda.nl, +31 (0)76 555 0200. The annual service charge reconciliation happens in January.

## Formal Complaints and Escalations

If a complaint is not resolved satisfactorily through FM, escalate to FM manager Jeroen van Dijk at +31 76 555 0101 or j.vandijk@vandalenfm.nl. If still unresolved, submit a formal complaint in writing to the building owner at eigenaar@meridian-breda.nl. Formal complaints are reviewed within 5 business days.

## Catering Orders

Meeting room catering and special event catering. Contact: Catering Culinaire, catering@meridian-breda.nl, at least 24 hours in advance.

## IT Credentials and WiFi Access

New employee WiFi credentials, password resets, Ethernet port activation. Contact: it@meridian-breda.nl with employee name, company name, and start date.

## Privacy and Data Requests

GDPR data access requests, privacy concerns, or questions about data processing. Contact: privacy@meridian-breda.nl. Data controller: Vastgoed Meridian B.V.

## Non-Building Topics

Restaurant recommendations, personal errands, car key copies, and other non-building topics are outside FM scope. Reception may be able to help with local recommendations.

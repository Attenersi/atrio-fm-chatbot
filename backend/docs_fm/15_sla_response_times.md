# Service Level Targets

## FM Response Times

Facility management aims to respond within the following timeframes:

URGENT priority (safety, flooding, fire, gas smell, sparking): initial response within 15 minutes, resolution target same day. Examples: water on electrical equipment, gas smell, smoke, sparking outlets, broken glass in walkways.

HIGH priority (no heating, power loss, major disruption): initial response within 1 hour, resolution target same day or next business day. Examples: no hot water on a floor, elevator malfunction, clogged toilet, power loss in a suite, broken locks.

NORMAL priority (dripping tap, broken equipment, flickering lights): initial response within 4 hours, resolution target within 5 business days.

LOW priority (cosmetic issues, minor convenience): initial response within 1 business day, resolution target within 10 business days.

These are targets, not guarantees. Complex issues requiring specialist contractors or parts ordering may take longer. FM will communicate expected timelines for each ticket.
